Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected and processed for the users of the Darts Checkout Grinder web application (hereinafter: the "App"). The App is a free, hobby-based project.

1. What data do we collect?
When you register and use the App, we collect and store the following information:

Email address: To identify your account and allow you to log in.

Password: Stored strictly securely and hashed. We cannot see or decrypt your password.

Nickname: To display you on the Global Leaderboard (optional, defaults to your account name).

Gameplay data: Your practice results, XP points, levels, and checkout statistics to help you track your progress.

2. How do we use your data?
The collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

To secure your personal account and save your gameplay (statistics, leveling).

To operate the Global Leaderboard.

Your data will never be sold, used for marketing purposes, or used to send unsolicited newsletters.

3. Where is the data stored (Third Parties)?
To operate the App and store data securely, we use Google Firebase services (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore). The data is stored on Google's highly secure, GDPR-compliant European/Global servers.

You can read more about Google's privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data retention and your rights
We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

Access the personal data we hold about you.

Modify your provided nickname within the App.

Permanently delete your account and all associated statistics (The Right to be Forgotten).

5. Account and Data Deletion (Contact)
Since this is a hobby project, an automated "Delete Account" button is currently under development. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all your data from our system, please send an email from your registered email address to: gyorimarcell49@gmail.com
Upon receiving your request, all your data will be manually and completely erased from the database.
